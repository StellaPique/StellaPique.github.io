The Stella Pique Experience is a social project made to help challenge me further socially and help end my lifelong loneliness. It would be the best thing I ever do in my entire life if I do go ahead with it and actually do what I set out to do, but it isn't as easy as that.

The premise is following: There will be Events / Episodes in which I go to a crowded place such as a festival, a flea market, or just a busy city such as Munich. While there, I have to hit a certain goal I set for myself which could be talking to 50 people, getting 10 signatures, getting a phone number, getting a certain reaction x times, et cetera.

These Episodes will be filmed by a cheap camera and wired lav microphone I bought for multiple reasons. Firstly, I want to track my progress for later Episodes and Seasons if it comes to that. Secondly, I want to be able to look back on what I did wrong so I can do it differently the next time. Thirdly, it requires courage to talk into a camera in public, which would also help me overcome some social fears.

Note: I will not be filming anyone without their permissions. I will also probably not be filming anyone with their permissions, as I do not plan to inform anyone I am filming. I hope the Lav doesn't give it away though, since I might use interaction audio.

All support, advice, and encouragement is greatly appreciated. At the time of writing this, it is not entirely sure if I will go ahead with it as has been planned for a while now, or if I will fail just like the last three times in the last two years. If anyone's reading this, wish me luck.

The first Event is supposed to be around the end of Project Stella Pique, which is the 31st of July. Exactly ten days after my 18th birthday. The time may vary due to me having to go on vacation, me not finding time, and me chickening out as I fight with my fears. 

If you are interested in watching my progress, I will have a Youtube channel dedicated to The Stella Pique Experience, though under a false name. I'll link it when it's time, if I succeed.

Only through arduos challenge can I become who I want to become. There is no amount of luck in the world that could help me.
