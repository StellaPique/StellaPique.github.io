This page serves a couple very different purposes. In the span of my doing I'll add more tabs up there (I have to learn how to do that first though) for the portfolio part, projects maybe, and other cool stuff. The blog is already handily there so that's lovely.

### A Little About Myself

I'm a really creative dude. I've written a book, and although it's crappy, It's kinda sweet I wrote it. And same thing with my poetry, and same thing with my old beginnery art, and pixel art, and my crappy games I made, and the crappy albums and soundtracks I've made. Same with all the other creative hobbies I've had or have. I love creating. It brings me joy to create and not just consume, and I feel mentally unwell if I do not. I have so much to say, so many ideas to share, so much inspiration to induce. I want to help people find this side in themselves as well, which makes part of my goal to impress people.

Having my own website channels basically everything artistic I've ever done (which as someone whose life is an artistic act itself is everything). I can post art once I've gotten better, I can write, I can have a tab for S.W.I.F.T (my narrative universe), I can link my music once I actually start publishing the things I do finally, I can share my games and progress if I ever get into a long term personal project. Most important of all, I can share TSPS updates and customs once the time comes.
