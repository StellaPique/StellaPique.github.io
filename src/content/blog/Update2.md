I've been working on the website more. As the title says.

##### What I did

I've started finding the structure I want to write my blogs and posts in on the website, so that's definitely great. The more consistency I have across these, the more proffesional it seems, and of course having an own way of doing stuff is the most beautiful gift an artist can give themselves. I've also finished a couple of the projects posts, and made a couple new designs for the ones which didn't have one yet. For the longest time Project SP's and Project Star's branding was the least of my concerns, so I never bothered to make it look too great or personalised. Now however, I went ahead and put some thought behind their designs, or at least behind Star's. The other one might still be prone to a redesign, but I'm not sure.

I've also looked more into the structure of stuff. I know how slugs work now. I would not be able to replicate it without help, but I get the general steps, and same with a lot of other stuff. I also get how the global CSS stuff works and all that, and while that might not be that complicated, It's nice to know. I always wondered how websites do their thing, and now I'm working on my own. Hopefully sometime in the future I can add more Stella touches to the UI, and rely less on the template.

It's coming together quite nicely so far though. It's not proffesional by any means but I feel like certain parts of it could pass, I just need to improve on what I have. The way up isn't as steep as one would think. Honestly, if you want to blog or build your own for whatever purpose just do it. Hit me up even, I'll help you through how I did it and set you on your way after cause I'm not that smart either yet.

##### What's next

Next I need to start integrating more stuff. I know I want to have a free ressource tab up there too for stuff like my get-ins, since having those hidden somewhere in the corners of my Hermes' Vault won't really help anyone else but myself in case someone wants to look into things I know stuff about. I also have to add Project Starlight and TSPS to the projects tab, as well as a couple other important ones. I also have to make the UI render more important stuff bigger, and less important stuff smaller by adding a project.data.importance variable or whatever and using that to calculate sizing. I also want to add variance in the top tabs so it doesn't look so stupid if there's like 20 tabs up there and it's just a long strip of text.
