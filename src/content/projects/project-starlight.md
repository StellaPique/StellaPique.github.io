Always known as Project Starlight, it serves as the twin project to Project Star, completing the couplet of self-improvement within Project SP.

##### Design

Originally, Starlight shared its design language with Project Star. While Star used blue as its accent colour, Starlight adopted a raspberry accent. Its primary identifier was a four-pointed star.

##### Purpose

Project Starlight is about building an identity, romanticising life without losing touch with reality, and developing the courage to truly enjoy living. It is not only about learning to appreciate yourself, but also about loving yourself and taking pride in the person you have become.

A simple question sits at the heart of the project: if you encountered yourself as a character in a story, would you admire who they are? If not, what would you want to change?

##### Core Values

The core values of Starlight are pride, living life to its fullest, and helping others become their favourite selves. This includes learning new hobbies, as it is essentially impossible to become who you're meant to become without passions and hobbies that change the way you see and enjoy life. Artistical hobbies are encouraged most.

##### CQ-X

The Customization-Quest-X is just what I call fashionistic hauls of wearables, meaning clothes, jewelry, and more. I try my best to save money where I can, so I plan out such hauls very meticulously and always at least a month if not several months before actually getting stuff. I think that is definitely the correct approach to it, since for me it has worked out perfectly so far. I don't have a single thing in my closet I don't wear regularly (excluding seasonals).

##### The Stella Pique Show

As corny as it sounds, this might become my biggest project ever, or at least one of the biggest. TSPS is supposed to launch around/on the 21-31st of July 2026, and be a social/vlogging project. It's just going to be me walking the streets of munich/big places in general and talking to people or messing with people lightheartedly. Of course as someone with a history of extreme anxiety, it won't just be extremely difficult, but another level of hardship beyond that to go out filming myself and socialising with strangers essentially. However, it will definitely be worth it. This will also not be the only thing I do, because I also aim to volunteer, feed the homeless (but definitely not film that part), and more on the side. I have much to gain from it identity-wise, and the world has much to gain from a full-potential Stella Pique.
