Always known as Project Starlight, it serves as the twin project to Project Star, completing the couplet of self-improvement within Project SP.

##### Design

Originally, Starlight shared its design language with Project Star. While Star used blue as its accent colour, Starlight adopted a raspberry accent. Its primary identifier was a four-pointed star.

##### Purpose

Project Starlight is about building an identity, romanticising life without losing touch with reality, and developing the courage to truly enjoy living. It is not only about learning to appreciate yourself, but also about loving yourself and taking pride in the person you have become.

A simple question sits at the heart of the project: if you encountered yourself as a character in a story, would you admire who they are? If not, what would you want to change?

##### Core Values

The core values of Starlight are pride, living life to its fullest, and helping others become their favourite selves.
