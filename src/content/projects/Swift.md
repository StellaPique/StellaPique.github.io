S.W.I.F.T is also known as Stories With Intertwining Fates & Timelines. **Find the dedicated Website *[here](https://stellapique.github.io/swift/)*.**

#### What & Why

This is unlike everything else not a self improvement or social project at all, but a Fictional Universe Project. It's comparable to the DCEU or MCU except it's my own thing. I made this project to help me be more creative while still being able to stick and glue all my creations together into one universe. That means whether I write fantasy, horror, romance or like your average corny superhero story or whatever you could think of, I am able to integrate it seemlessly into the SWIFTYVERSE. A lot of details like time, ages of characters, some uninteresting worldbuilding aspects and such are kept ambiguos so that the reader and the writer can immerse themselves better into the story and have more freedom in writing. Locations and use of Technology, Powers, Magic (the trio known as Augmentations, and the users known as Powerlings) vary highly.

#### How I Use It

S.W.I.F.T serves as the setting for nearly all of my creative work. During the summer holidays of 9th grade, I wrote a novel titled The Crimson Order. I also spent some time writing connected bite-sized (~300-word) short stories under the name S.W.I.F.T Stories, alongside creating pixel art and several music projects set within the universe.

Going forward, I plan to continue expanding the universe through additional stories, artwork, and eventually comics as I improve my artistic skills. Developing a game set in the S.W.I.F.T universe is also a long-term goal, although it will have to wait until I have more time available.
