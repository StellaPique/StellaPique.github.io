# Time Phases

There's been a couple major time phases in the project, although since of the latest I haven't been as motivated to do those anymore, not sure why.

##### 5% Phase: The First Stretch

First CQ (Customization Quest) initiated here. Started taking better care of hygiene. Started eating better. Gathered up the courage to give Lux & Pinkie letters despite my extreme social anxiety. A quite significant start to what was at the time Project Star.

##### 15% Phase: Ascension Of Productivity

Started taking even better care of myself. Another CQ, started sleeping better, started being slightly more open to people to try and beat my anxiety, though only when spoken to. Miscallaneous small goals. WARRS10% was initiated.

##### 19% Phase

I defeated my anxiety for the biggest part in the span of a week using a very advanced tactic called "humiliating myself and bullshitting my way through social life". I also had a major motivator being my crush on a girl for three years at the time, and lifelong self hatred and spite.

##### 27% Phase

I wrote an 78k word (~350 Page) book in the span of 43 days. I also received **The Mark** in this time. S.W.I.F.T (Stories With Intertwining Fates & TImelines) was made along with the creation of The Crimson Order, the book. CQ3 & CQ3.5 were initiated.
