Always known as Project Star. Used to be the main project before it split off into two, the second one being Starlight which evolved into its own thing, both being umbrella'd by Project SP.

##### Design

Project Star was the project that started the biggest most important phase of my life for now. It originally featured a pink colour scheme banner similar to the Project Mayhem/Ascend/Syndicate ones but with a star in the middle. Due to the recent branding and rebranding of all existing projects, the design has been changed into something that more so reflects the soul thereof.

##### Purpose

Its purpose in Project SP is being the project of reflective personal development. This includes the kinds of things you would find in stoicism and mindful self-improvement culture. Most subprojects of it have been "Starlets". A Starlet is a progress tracking and note taking tool, each having its own purpose (such as the Golden Starlet, or the Silver Starlet for example). The Clock, and the Starlets serving as reminders work together in collaboration to help me stay on the rigth path and motivate me to keep going forward. It's a very simple thing on the surface, sure, but the amount of mileage you get from merely remembering to do your daily habits, from remembering what you have to do and forcing yourself not to procrastinate, from refraining taking the lazy choice once in a while because you remember the project and what you've been working for.

It also has the purpose of helping with time tracking as it includes The Clock counting down days until the 31st of July 2026. You never really notice how quickly time passes by before you start counting it. 438 days left seems like it was yesterday. The 27% phase seems like it was yesterday. It really makes you appreciate life and sit down to think once in a while.

##### Core Values

The core values of Project Star are Hope, Ambition, and Time-Management. Hope shows in the perseverance of continuing to improve upon the same thing, as much as it rejects you, and as much as you lose motivation to. Ambition shows in the goals I set for myself in the previous time phases, most of which I reached successfully. Time-Management shows in the existence of the clock.
