Always known as Project Star. Used to be the main project before it split off into two, the second one being Starlight which evolved into its own thing, both being umbrella'd by Project SP.

##### Design

Project Star was the project that started the biggest most important phase of my life for now. It originally featured a pink colour scheme banner similar to the Project Mayhem/Ascend/Syndicate ones but with a star in the middle. Due to the recent branding and rebranding of all existing projects, the design has been changed into something that more so reflects the soul thereof.

##### Purpose

Its purpose in Project SP is being the project of interior improvement. This includes the kinds of things you would find in stoicism and mindful self-improvement culture. Most subprojects of it have been "Starlets". A Starlet is a progress tracking and note taking tool, each having its own purpose (such as the Golden Starlet, or the Silver Starlet for example). 

It also has the purpose of helping with time tracking as it includes The Clock counting down days until the 31st of July 2026.

##### Core Values

The core values of Project Star are Hope, Ambition, and Time-Management. Hope shows in the perseverance of continuing to improve upon the same thing, as much as it rejects you, and as much as you lose motivation to. Ambition shows in the goals I set for myself in the previous time phases, most of which I reached successfully. Time-Management shows in the existence of the clock.

##### Collaboration

If you're in a similar headspace about the self improvement thing I wouldn't mind company. After all, I couldn't have come as far as I did without support.
